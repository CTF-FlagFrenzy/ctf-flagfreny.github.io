# About FlagFrenzy

## Our Mission

FlagFrenzy is a community-driven Capture The Flag (CTF) platform dedicated to making cybersecurity education accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone. We believe that hands-on learning through CTF challenges is one of the most effective ways to develop practical security skills.

## What We Do

- **Host Regular CTF Competitions**: From beginner-friendly events to advanced challenges
- **Provide Learning Resources**: Tutorials, write-ups, and documentation to help you improve
- **Build Community**: Connect security enthusiasts from around the world

## Our Team

FlagFrenzy was founded by a group of cybersecurity professionals and enthusiasts who wanted to create a welcoming environment for people to learn and practice security skills.

## Get Involved

Interested in contributing to FlagFrenzy? We're always looking for:

- Challenge creators
- Content writers
- Community moderators
- Open source contributors

Feel free to reach out to us at [contact@flagfrenzy.com](mailto:contact@flagfrenzy.com) or join our Discord server.

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all. Please review our [Code of Conduct](/docs/code-of-conduct) before participating.
